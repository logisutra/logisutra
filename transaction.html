📦 International Incoterm Guidelines (2020)
EXW
Ex Works (Named Place)

You must package goods and make them ready at your warehouse. The buyer absorbs all transport costs and hazards.

FOB
Free On Board (Origin Port)

Your responsibility ends once the goods safely cross the ship's rail at the export port terminal.

CIF
Cost, Insurance & Freight

You are contractually required to pay for the marine freight transit and basic cargo insurance to the port of entry.

DDP
Delivered Duty Paid

You hold ultimate liability, handling all clearances, local import taxes, and direct deliveries to the buyer's destination.
